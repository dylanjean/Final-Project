The Developers

All four of the developers of this application completed a 24 week intensive bootcamp for data analysis and visualization through the University of Pennsylvania. This bootcamp prepared them to work with Python, SQL, Javascript, CSS, Tableau, Plotly and several others. This application was submitted as the final project for the course in December of 2020. All the developers would like to recognize their instructor, Ed Brennan and ta, Madison Leopold, to whom a great deal of thanks is owed. As well, a huge thank you to all the fuzzy support animals who made it possible to smile at 2am in front of the computer after multiple failed attempts.
